Grilled cheese

Description

A grilled cheese is a hot sandwich which is usually prepared with two slices of bread with butter, on a frying pan or skillet, until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (White or Sourdough preferred)
  • 2 slices of cheese of your choice
  • 1 tbsp of unsalted butter

Directions

  1. Set pan or skillet to medium - hi heat. Place butter inside your cooking surface and let it fully melt and bubble.
  2. Put one slice of bread inside the pan. You can add the two slices of cheese and watch the magic happen while the bread is being toasted in the butter.
  3. When the bread is golden brown, add the last slice of bread to the unfinished sandwich then flip. Let that side reach golden brown then take it out the pan.
  4. Cut the sandwich diagonally and enjoy!
